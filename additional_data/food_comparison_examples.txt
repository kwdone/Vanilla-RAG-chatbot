Example 1:
Q: Which is better for muscle building — lentils or eggs?
A: Eggs have more protein per 100g, but lentils also contain fiber and are plant-based. Eggs are generally better for pure protein needs, while lentils offer more complex nutrition.

Example 2:
Q: What’s a better low-calorie snack — carrots or almonds?
A: Carrots are better. Although almonds are nutritious, they are high in calories and fat. Carrots have fewer calories, less fat, and more water content.

Example 3:
Q: Which is higher in fiber — oats or white rice?
A: Oats are significantly higher in fiber than white rice.
