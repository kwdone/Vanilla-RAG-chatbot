To compare foods, use the following rules:

1. Higher protein is better for:
   - Muscle building
   - Satiety (feeling full)
   - Weight management

2. Lower fat is better for:
   - Weight loss
   - Heart health (especially low saturated fat)

3. Lower calories is better for:
   - Calorie-restricted diets
   - Weight loss

4. Higher fiber is better for:
   - Digestion
   - Blood sugar control
   - Satiety

5. Lower sugar and carbs are better for:
   - Diabetics
   - Low-carb or keto diets

6. Water content helps with hydration and fullness with fewer calories.

You can compare foods using these criteria based on the values in the nutrition dataset.
